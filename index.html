Our Heritage

The Legendary Flavors of Bihar

Champaran Chulha brings the legendary flavors of Bihar's traditional handi cooking to Vadodara. Each curry is prepared using the age-old technique of sealing the handi with dough and cooking it slowly over a chulha.

This method locks in the aroma, enhances the spices, and delivers a rich, smoky experience you won't forget. It's not just food; it's a ritual of patience and passion.
