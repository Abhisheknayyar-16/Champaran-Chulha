Our Heritage

The Legendary Flavors of Bihar

Champaran Chulha brings the legendary flavors of Bihar's traditional handi cooking to Vadodara.

Each curry is prepared using the age-old technique of sealing the handi with dough and cooking it slowly over a chulha.

This method locks in aroma, enhances spices and delivers a rich smoky experience.
